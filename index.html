Who We Are

A Firm Built on Straight Talk

At A.D. Dandala Attorneys At Law, straight talk guides everything we do. It is how we converse with clients, colleagues, courts and even our adversaries. It means we pursue issues head-on, rather than detouring into legalese.

We cut through the confusion and find simple solutions to help our clients succeed. We build the best team for each client by using a multidisciplinary approach that combines knowledge, experience and individual strengths.
